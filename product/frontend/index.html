PRODUCT SCOPE

What is implemented today

Transparent prototype disclosure

Implemented in this local prototype

  • Offline data validation, canonicalization, and pre-trained inference.
  • 30/60/90-day scenario forecasts with P10/P50/P90 ranges.
  • Constraint-aware budget allocation and a local decision ledger.
  • Deterministic decision briefs tied to sealed forecast evidence.

Production roadmap — not implemented here

  • Multi-tenant identity, permissions, and tenant data isolation.
  • Managed cloud deployment, asynchronous orchestration, and observability.
  • Governed model registry, feature store, and scheduled retraining.
  • Reviewed attribution integrations and portfolio-level calibration at scale.

This is a single-user local product prototype. It does not claim production authentication, multi-tenancy, cloud operations, or live LLM availability.

TRUST CENTER

Data health and backtest

OPERATIONAL RISK

Model boundaries

  • Meta revenue semantics are derived from validated canonical quality flags and surfaced in Data health.
  • Platform attribution is the source of truth; Horizon does not claim causal lift.
  • Budget changes are conditional forecasts; wide ranges or insufficient calibration require validation before action.
  • Decision briefs are deterministic by default; live LLM narration is localhost-only opt-in and never changes numbers.
